# Features & roadmap

  • Create and execute your strategies in minutes.
  • Available as a Spring boot starter (opens new window).
  • Connect to several cryptocurrency exchanges thanks to XChange.
  • User, accounts, and balances management with real-time market data.
  • Buy / Sell market & limit orders.
  • Automatic position management (with stop gain & stop loss rules).
  • Dry mode & backtesting.
  • Extensively tested and documented.

Our Roadmap is available on Github (opens new window).
