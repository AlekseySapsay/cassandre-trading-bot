      # Features & roadmap

      # Features

      • Create and execute your strategy in seconds.
      • Available as a Spring boot starter (opens new window).
      • Connect to several cryptocurrency exchanges (thanks to XChange (opens new window)).
      • User, accounts, and balances management with real-time market data.
      • Buy / Sell market & limit orders.
      • Automatic position management.
      • Dry mode & backtesting.
      • Extensively tested (opens new window) and documented.
