Cassandre

An open-source framework that makes it easy to create your Java crypto trading bot

Quick Start

Get into trading with Minimum fuss

Available as a Spring boot starter, Cassandre takes care of exchange connections, accounts, orders, trades, and positions, so you can focus on building your strategy.

Create your strategy in minutes

Just code when you want to take short/long positions, set the rules, and we take care of everything (buying, selling, rules management, orders, trades, and tickers).

Dry mode & backtesting made easy

We provide a dry mode to simulate exchange replies to your orders, so you can make tests. We also provide a spring boot starter to backtest your bot on historical data.
